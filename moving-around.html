Transportation Tips

  • The roads in Santorini can be dangerous due to poor organization. Exercise caution while traveling.
  • Always wear helmets if you're riding a scooter or ATV.
  • Plan your transportation in advance, especially if you're visiting remote villages.
  • Consider renting a vehicle for more flexibility in exploring the island.
  • Check bus schedules and routes for efficient travel between villages.
